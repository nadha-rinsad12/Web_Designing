Children’s Books

BookShop publishes many hundreds of stories, and novels
drawn from the rich cultural heritage of Eastern lands. As with all
great stories, they work on many levels – entertaining the listener,
while imparting a teaching message, an element that is in itself
of profound value. These editions have the added benefit of being
beautifully illustrated by award-winning illustrators from around
the world.
